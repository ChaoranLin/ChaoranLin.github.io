Biography

I am a second-year Master student majoring in Mechanical Engineering at School of Mechanical Science and Engineering of HUST, supervised by A.P. Songxin Shi. My research interests mainly focus on the application of intelligent algorithms and advanced manufacturing methods in the field of intelligent manufacturing.I received the China National Scholarship in 2019.

what I am doing recently ?

  • The effect of temperature on the cutting of flexible substrates with low power laser was studied.(Updated on November 2, 2019).
  • The cutting property of low-power lasers on flexible bases was studied. The influence of some important processing parameters on cutting performance was obtained by orthogonal experiment.(Updated on October 8, 2019).
  • The structure of the bionic hand and the application of the flexible sensor in the bionic hand are studied.(Updated on September 8, 2019).

Projects

 
 
 
 
 

Design of college dormitory system integrating dormitory control and micro-social

Project Manager

May 2019 – Aug 2019 China
  • It has realized the functions of electrical control, safety precaution, health care and other aspects of the college student dormitory.See the video.
  • The concept of “dormitory circle” was put forward, and some function-driven social applications with precise adaptation were designed.
  • The function of the mobile terminal application is used as the carrier, and the application design is implemented by using the Android Studio software.
  • The solar power supply module is designed by using the MPPT algorithm, and is provided with overvoltage and undervoltage protection.
 
 
 
 
 

Design of surface defect inspection system for sawn timbers

Project Finisher

Apr 2017 – Jun 2018 China
  • The hardware system design of the defect detection system was carried out, and the defect image sample library was collected and prepared.
  • Image preprocessing, image segmentation, and post-segment morphological processing are completed to realize image extraction of defective areas.
  • The defect characteristics were determined, the feature parameters were extracted, and the BP neural network classifier was trained and tested.
  • Use LabVIEW as the main software to write the software system, and call MATLAB for data analysis and pattern recognition.See the video.
